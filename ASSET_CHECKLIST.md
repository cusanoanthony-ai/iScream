## Image Source Guidelines

- Only use photos owned by I Scream Yogurt or explicitly approved by the owner.
- Prefer original high-resolution files over screenshots from social media.
- Record candidate sources in `docs/photo-source-log.md`.
- Update alt text in `src/data/images.ts` when photos are added.
